About

Computer Society of Anna university is a professional club of Anna university. Our purpose is to advance the practice and application of computer science and information technology in various fields. Our club conducts numerous workshops, regular classes and other educational activities at all levels. We come together to provide a forum to standardise professionally and advances discipline of technology.
